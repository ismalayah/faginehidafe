Will your Pottermore Patronus be in Hogwarts Legacy?

Publish date: 2024-06-27

1. Can you use the Patronus charm in Hogwarts Legacy?

No, you cannot use the Patronus charm in Hogwarts Legacy. This has been confirmed by the Warner Bros Games Support Twitter account.

2. Does Patronus transfer to Hogwarts Legacy?

Unfortunately, a Patronus from a Harry Potter Fan Club account cannot be linked to Hogwarts Legacy.

3. Does Pottermore link to Hogwarts Legacy?

The official website of Hogwarts Legacy redirects you to The Harry Potter Fan Club, which is the official website of Wizarding World. If you have your Wizarding World (formerly known as Pottermore) account, you can link it to your Hogwarts Legacy game.
