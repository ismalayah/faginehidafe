Insomniac Reveals Details For EDC Las Vegas 2017

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Coinciding with Ultra Music Festival’s big lineup reveal yesterday, Insomniac Events has announced the first round of details for Electric Daisy Carnival 2017. The EDC brand has surged in 2016, with the flagship edition serving as one of the best events of festival season, while EDC Orlando saw some major expansions this year, too. With the new details that have surfaced, it looks like Insomniac intends to keep the ball rolling next year.

Recommended Videos

EDC Las Vegas is scheduled to return to the Las Vegas Motor Speedway in 2017 for three days of musical madness, running from June 16-18. Insomniac is gearing up for one of their biggest events yet and EDC Las Vegas 2017 will be preceded by EDC Week, which will occur at various nightclubs and hotels around the city starting on June 12 and concluding a day after the festival ends on June 19.

General admission, Early owl and VIP passes will go on sale Monday, November 28 at 12pm PT, with packages ranging from $335-$699 plus taxes and fees. Insomniac is offering payment plans on tickets, while shuttle passes and premier shuttle passes will also be available to get fans to the festival grounds.
