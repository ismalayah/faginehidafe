Taron Egerton Becomes The MCUs Wolverine In Incredible New Art

Publish date: 2024-06-15

It’s almost impossible to see anyone besides Hugh Jackman playing Wolverine, but eventually it’s going to happen. So why not talk about it? And if you have the skills, Photoshop it?

Recommended Videos

One famous fan has now used those talents to create some support for the latest celeb rumored to be inheriting those metal claws: Taron Egerton. Never one to let their fanbase down, popular digital artist Boss Logic has done it again. This time? Wolverine’s successor.

Recently, comic book writer Mark Millar offered up his prediction that the next actor to portray the vicious hero will be none other than Egerton, Jackman’s Eddie and the Eagle co-star. And now, the artist has shared his quirky take on the latest rumors about who the next Logan will be.

First, he posted an edit that’s originally a promotional pic from Eddie with the addition of a Wolverine mask, but it’s still adorable as hell. After that, Boss Logic then shared some proper fan art of how Egerton could look as the iconic mutant.

When asked about his possible future as the most famous X-Man, Egerton recently told Digital Spy that as far as he knows, it’s just a rumor.

“That one I think is just a rumour. As far as I’m aware it’s unfounded. I don’t know if I’m quite grisly enough.

He didn’t completely shut it all down, however, saying:

“I mean if the people at Marvel think I am then great, let’s go. But I think there are probably better candidates. But I would love to be involved with that world somewhere.”

Take all this with a grain of salt, of course. Even Daniel Radcliffe once joked about being the next Wolverine, and received the fan art treatment, too. It wasn’t bad, but seeing Harry Potter as Weapon X? No thanks. I’m still trying to digest Cedric Diggory being our next Batman, so I’m not sure my imagination can handle that.

With all the MCU news coming from this year’s SDCC, it’s almost possible to get superhero overload right now, but tell us, who has your vote as the next Wolverine? Does fan favorite Tom Hardy get your nomination, or is there a lesser known actor you’d like to see in the role? Spill it in the comments section down below.
